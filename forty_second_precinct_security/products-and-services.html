Brief History
  1. Founded in 1992, as Max Security & Residential Patrols.
  2. Due to increasing demand, the company evolved to offer permanent and temporary on-site security officers.
  3. Continued expansion of both the response and guard’s divisions, including close liaison and involvement with all local crime prevention bodies, allowed the company to become the dominant Security Service in the area.
  4. By early 1997 the company was very well established and had already earned an extremely good reputation amongst the community who knew they could rely on Max Security to provide an efficient and reliable service.
  5. In 2002 a name change was applied for and 42nd Precinct Security was born. All clients accepted and embraced the name change and continued to be valuable customers of this company.
  6. The ownership of the company changed March 2004.The company is wholly owned by Allan Millard (MBA), who is the incumbent Managing Director, and has been at the helm ever since.
  7. In 2005, the Electronics Solutions Division was established, to to satisfy the ever-growing need of Intrusion Detection Systems in households and enterprise buildings.

Armed Response
  • Armed Response and monitoring of Alarm systems is included in our business offering.
  • We have an equipped control room with the latest technology to enable us to receive a multitude of alarm signals from client alarms.
  • These options will include but not limited to:
    • Alarm
    • Panic
    • Medical Emergency
    • Duress code
    • AC Loss
    • AC Restore
    • Low Battery
    • Openings
    • Closings

We can transmit any, or all, alarm signals to relevant clients, via SMS or GPRS This affords our clients early notification on alarm signals transmitted by their own alarms. The Armed Response vehicle is notified to respond to the address by the Controller on duty in the control room. This after the necessary calls are made to ensure a real response situation. This would ensure that the vehicle Reaction Officer would know about the activation so that the officer responds in a coordinated manner and will arrive within three to seven minutes of being dispatched by the control room.
All vehicles are equipped with
  • Satellite tracking
  • GPS - All clients are pre-loaded on the GPS to ensure a quick as possible response time.
  • Base Radios
  • Cell phones

There is a rendezvous service for armed response clients. This does not have a call out fee associated unless the client has a specific daily requirement, or whether it be adhoc or regular. This is a point that must be discussed to understand how to quote and cost for it
Armed Response vehicles are stationed in and around the Ferndale, Randburg area and are moved to various points at different times determined by the control room when there are no alarms to be attended to, where the vehicles are required to respond to.

IP or GPRS technology

We provide and enhance your experience with your home or business alarm by offering IP or GPRS managed technology for you, to manage and control your alarm/s via your cell phone. This technology allows you to monitor and control your own alarm system, down to partition and even zone level, even when you are not on the premises. This allows you to have the power in your hands. All historical records are available on the platform in the form of data or SMS, allowing you to scan history reports, when there were openings or closings, or even alarms. This system will even notify you of your battery backup status. Our control room is further equipped with back-up power in terms of UPS’s and a back-up generator.
